JW Reminder Hub

Meeting Assignment Organizer

About

JW Reminder Hub helps you communicate meeting assignments effectively. Send professional reminders instantly to your entire congregation, ensuring everyone stays informed about their responsibilities.

  • Pre-built templates for Life and Ministry School, Public Talks, Sound, and Attendant schedules
  • Customizable templates to fit your congregation's style
  • Configurable roles for unique responsibilities
  • Instant text message notifications
  • Professional, consistent communication

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I send reminders?

Select a notification type (Life and Ministry School, Public Talk, Sound, or Attendant), fill in the assignments, and tap "Prepare Text Message" to generate a professionally formatted reminder that you can send via Messages.

Can I customize the message templates?

Yes! Navigate to Templates from the main screen to create custom templates that match your congregation's communication style.

Can I add custom roles for my congregation?

Absolutely. Go to Settings > Role Configuration to customize the roles and responsibilities specific to your congregation's needs.

What permissions does the app need?

JW Reminder Hub requests access to your Contacts (to select people for assignments) and Notifications (to send reminders). These permissions help you coordinate effectively.

Is my data private?

Yes. All data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or share any of your information.
