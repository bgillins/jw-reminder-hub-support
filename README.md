## About JW Reminder Hub

JW Reminder Hub helps coordinators communicate meeting assignments effectively. The app provides:

- Pre-built templates for Life and Ministry School, Public Talks, Sound, and Attendant schedules
- Customizable templates to fit your congregation's style
- Configurable roles for unique responsibilities
- Instant text message notifications
- Professional, consistent communication

## Privacy

JW Reminder Hub is committed to user privacy:

- All data is stored locally on the user's device
- No data is collected, transmitted, or shared
- No third-party analytics or tracking
- Full privacy policy available at the link above
